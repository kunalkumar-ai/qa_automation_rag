To: All ElectroTech Business Partners  
Date: January 15, 2025  
Subject: Updated B2B Payment Terms and Procedures  

Dear Valued Partner,

We are pleased to inform you of the updated payment terms and procedures at ElectroTech, effective immediately, to streamline and enhance our business transactions. Our commitment is to maintain transparency and efficiency in all our operations, and these updates are an integral part of that promise.

The standard payment term for all business-to-business transactions with ElectroTech is net-30. This means that payment is due in full within thirty days from the date of the invoice. To encourage prompt payment, we are also offering an early payment discount. Partners who settle their invoices within ten days of the invoice date are eligible for a 2% discount on the total amount due. This early payment option is designed to benefit those who can expedite their payment processes, ensuring a mutually beneficial financial relationship.

ElectroTech accepts several methods of payment to accommodate the diverse needs of our partners. The accepted payment methods include wire transfers, ACH transfers, and major credit cards. We recommend using wire or ACH transfers for larger transactions due to their reliability and efficiency. For credit card payments, please ensure that any transaction-related charges are covered by the payer to avoid discrepancies in the invoice amount.

In the event of any invoice disputes, ElectroTech has established a comprehensive dispute resolution process to ensure that all concerns are addressed promptly and effectively. Partners are encouraged to contact our Accounts Receivable department via email at ar@electrotech.com within five business days of receiving the invoice. Please include the invoice number, product model (such as ET-X200, ET-X300, ET-S100, or ET-P50), and a detailed description of the dispute. Our team is committed to responding to all inquiries within two business days, aiming to resolve any discrepancies in a timely manner.

Please note that these updates are part of the ElectroTech Financial Policy Revision 2025-01, in compliance with Regulation 2025-FP-ET. We believe these measures will enhance our financial interactions and strengthen our business relationships.

For further inquiries or assistance, please do not hesitate to reach out to our customer service team at support@electrotech.com. We value your partnership and are committed to providing you with the highest level of service.

Thank you for your attention to these updates and for your continued collaboration.

Sincerely,

James L. Carter  
Chief Financial Officer
