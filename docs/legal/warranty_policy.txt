ElectroTech is pleased to offer a comprehensive 24-month warranty on our range of electronic products, including models ET-X200, ET-X300, ET-S100, and ET-P50. This warranty is designed to provide our valued customers with peace of mind, ensuring that any manufacturing defects or malfunctions are addressed promptly and efficiently.

The scope of this warranty covers all defects in material and workmanship under normal use during the warranty period. It is important to note that the 24-month period commences from the date of purchase, as indicated on the original sales receipt. During this period, ElectroTech will repair or replace, at no charge, products or parts of a product that prove defective under normal use and maintenance. Should a replacement be necessary, it will be with a new or refurbished product or part that is equivalent to the original.

To initiate a warranty claim, customers must first contact our customer service department at warranty@electrotech.com to obtain a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number. This number is crucial for processing any returns and must be clearly indicated on the shipping label. The product should be returned in its original packaging, along with the proof of purchase and a detailed description of the defect. Upon receipt, our technical team will conduct a thorough inspection to verify the claim. Customers can expect communication regarding the status of their claim within ten business days of receipt.

It is important for customers to understand the exclusions that apply to this warranty. The warranty does not cover damages resulting from misuse, unauthorized alterations, or repairs made by individuals or organizations not authorized by ElectroTech. Additionally, normal wear and tear, damage caused by accidents or natural disasters, and issues arising from improper installation are not included under this warranty. Specific attention should be paid to ensure compatibility with regional electrical standards, as damages resulting from failure to adhere to Regulation Number 2025-ETR-56 will also void this warranty.
