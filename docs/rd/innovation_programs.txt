To: ElectroTech Employees  
Subject: ElectroTech Innovation Partnerships Initiative

Date: January 15, 2025

As part of ElectroTech's commitment to fostering innovation and maintaining our position at the forefront of the electronics industry, we are pleased to announce the launch of our comprehensive Innovation Partnerships Initiative. This initiative is designed to strengthen our collaborative efforts through three key programs: the University Collaboration Programme, Startup Accelerator, and Joint Development Agreements. Each of these programs aims to harness external expertise, drive technological advancements, and create mutually beneficial relationships that align with our strategic goals.

The University Collaboration Programme is targeted at establishing robust partnerships with leading academic institutions. We will focus on research projects that complement our core product lines, such as our ET-X200 and ET-X300 models, which are pivotal in the consumer electronics market. By engaging with top universities, we aim to tap into groundbreaking research, facilitate knowledge exchange, and support the development of new technologies that align with our future product roadmaps. Interested faculty and researchers are encouraged to submit proposals by March 31, 2025, to partnerships@electrotech.com.

Our Startup Accelerator is designed to nurture innovative startups that are developing next-generation technologies relevant to our business. The accelerator offers financial investment, mentorship, and access to ElectroTech's extensive network. Emphasis will be placed on startups that show promise in enhancing the capabilities of our ET-S100 and ET-P50 product lines, which are integral to our sustainable technology strategy. Applications for the next cohort are open until April 30, 2025. Prospective startups should contact accelerator@electrotech.com for application guidelines and further details.

Joint Development Agreements (JDAs) allow us to collaborate closely with industry partners to co-develop new technologies and products. These agreements are particularly beneficial for projects requiring complex integration of systems, such as those in compliance with Regulation 2025/78/EU pertaining to electronic device energy efficiency. Our JDAs aim to leverage partner strengths and market insights, ensuring that our products remain cutting-edge and compliant with regulatory frameworks. Interested parties should reach out to jda@electrotech.com to discuss potential collaborations.

As we move forward with these initiatives, we underscore the importance of innovation, transparency, and mutual benefit in all our partnerships. Together, we can accelerate the development of pioneering technologies that not only meet but exceed market expectations. For further information or queries regarding the Innovation Partnerships Initiative, please contact our partnerships team at partnerships@electrotech.com.

We look forward to your active participation and collaboration in these endeavors.
