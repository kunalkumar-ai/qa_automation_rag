As part of ElectroTech's commitment to transparency and innovation, we are pleased to provide an overview of the frequently asked questions regarding our product roadmap for the upcoming year, 2025. This document aims to address key inquiries concerning our forthcoming product lines, the feature request process, the beta testing programme, and our policy on information covered by non-disclosure agreements (NDAs).

In 2025, ElectroTech is excited to launch several new models designed to meet diverse consumer needs and preferences. Among these, the ET-X200 and ET-X300 will lead our flagship series, featuring enhanced AI integration and superior connectivity options, tailored to both professional and personal use. Additionally, the ET-S100 and ET-P50 models will cater to more budget-conscious consumers without compromising on quality and performance. These products are scheduled for release in the second and third quarters of the year, respectively.

To ensure our products continually evolve to meet customer expectations, we have instituted a formal feature request process. Stakeholders and consumers can submit requests via our dedicated portal, accessible through our corporate website. Each submission will be reviewed by our product development team, with evaluations based on feasibility, alignment with company strategy, and potential market impact. For further assistance, please contact our support team at featurerequests@electrotech.com.

ElectroTech also runs an exclusive beta testing programme that allows select users to experience and provide feedback on our upcoming products before their official release. Participation in this programme is by invitation only, and it requires potential testers to complete an application process. Selected participants will be required to sign a non-disclosure agreement to protect proprietary information. This ensures that all feedback remains confidential until the products are officially launched. Inquiries regarding the beta programme can be directed to betaprogramme@electrotech.com.

Regarding information protected by non-disclosure agreements, ElectroTech strictly adheres to all legal and regulatory requirements. As such, any data or insights shared under an NDA are considered highly confidential and are governed by regulation number 2025/ET-NDA/482. Unauthorized disclosure is subject to company policy and legal repercussions. Employees and partners are reminded to review our confidentiality guidelines, detailed under policy revision number 2025-03, to ensure compliance.

ElectroTech remains dedicated to fostering innovation and delivering exceptional electronic products. For further information regarding our product roadmap or related inquiries, please reach out to roadmap@electrotech.com. We appreciate your ongoing support and commitment to our brand's values and vision.
