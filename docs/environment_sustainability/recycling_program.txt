To: ElectroTech Employees  
From: Environmental Compliance Department  
Date: January 15, 2025  
Subject: Implementation and Overview of ElectroTech WEEE Recycling Program

As part of our ongoing commitment to environmental sustainability and compliance with the Waste Electrical and Electronic Equipment (WEEE) Directive 2012/19/EU, ElectroTech is pleased to announce the launch of our comprehensive WEEE Recycling Program. This program is designed to facilitate the responsible recycling of our electronic products, namely the ET-X200, ET-X300, ET-S100, and ET-P50, thereby minimizing our environmental footprint and promoting sustainable practices.

The ElectroTech WEEE Recycling Program will feature a network of designated drop-off points strategically located across key urban centers to ensure accessibility for our customers. These drop-off points will serve as convenient locations where customers can recycle their end-of-life electronics safely and responsibly. Our initial rollout includes pilot locations in New York, Los Angeles, and Chicago, with plans to expand to additional cities by the end of 2025.

In line with regulation number 2025/03-WEEE, ElectroTech will also be implementing a take-back scheme. This initiative allows customers to return their old electronics directly to us when purchasing new products from our range. The take-back scheme will be seamlessly integrated into our existing logistics operations, ensuring a smooth transition for customers and adherence to our sustainability goals.

Furthermore, our program emphasizes the recycling of packaging materials. We are committed to reducing packaging waste by employing recyclable and biodegradable materials for all new products released in 2025. Customers are encouraged to return used packaging to the designated drop-off points for recycling. This initiative supports our objective of minimizing landfill contributions and enhancing the circular economy.

ElectroTech has set ambitious annual targets to ensure the effectiveness of our WEEE Recycling Program. For the year 2025, we aim to achieve a 40% recycling rate for all products sold, with a progressive increase to 60% by 2027. These targets are aligned with our goal to lead the industry in sustainable practices and to comply with national and international environmental regulations.

We invite all employees to actively participate in and promote the WEEE Recycling Program. Your contribution is crucial to achieving our environmental objectives and ensuring the success of this initiative. For further details or inquiries regarding the program, please contact the Environmental Compliance Department at envirocompliance@electrotech.com.

Thank you for your continued support and dedication to fostering a sustainable future. Together, we can make a significant impact on reducing electronic waste and promoting a healthier planet.
