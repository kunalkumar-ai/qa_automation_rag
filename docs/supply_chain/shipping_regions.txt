ElectroTech is committed to delivering our high-quality electronics to customers around the globe with efficiency and reliability. As part of this commitment, we have outlined our shipping coverage policy, detailing supported countries, restricted regions, courier partnerships, and tracking information, effective as of January 1, 2025.

ElectroTech proudly ships our innovative product lines, including the ET-X200, ET-X300, ET-S100, and ET-P50 models, to a broad array of countries across North America, Europe, Asia, and Oceania. In North America, our coverage includes the United States, Canada, and Mexico. Our European market encompasses the United Kingdom, Germany, France, Italy, and Spain, among others. In Asia, we service major markets including Japan, South Korea, China, and India. Our operations extend to Australia and New Zealand in Oceania. 

However, due to various logistical constraints and regulatory considerations, certain regions remain restricted. This includes countries under international sanctions or embargoes, in compliance with United Nations regulations. For instance, we are currently unable to ship to North Korea, Syria, and Iran. Additionally, territories with unstable political climates or insufficient infrastructure may also face restrictions. We regularly review these restrictions in alignment with international trade policies and will update our policy accordingly.

To ensure timely and secure delivery of our products, ElectroTech partners with leading global courier services, including DHL Express, FedEx, and UPS. These partnerships enable us to provide our customers with a range of shipping options, from standard delivery to expedited services, depending on their specific needs and geographic location. Each shipment is handled with the utmost care to maintain the integrity of our products during transit.

ElectroTech values transparency and convenience for our customers. Consequently, we offer comprehensive tracking information for all shipments. Upon dispatch, customers receive a tracking number via email, allowing them to monitor the progress of their package in real-time through the courier's online tracking system. This service ensures that customers are kept informed of their order status from the moment it leaves our warehouse to its final delivery destination.

For further inquiries regarding shipping coverage or specific regional policies, customers are encouraged to contact our customer service team at shippinginquiries@electrotech.com. We are dedicated to providing exceptional service and will continue to enhance our shipping strategies to better serve our global community.
