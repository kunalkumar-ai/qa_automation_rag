Subject: Spare Parts Availability and Ordering Process for Supported Models  
Date: March 15, 2025

As part of our ongoing commitment to provide exemplary service to our customers, we are pleased to detail the spare parts availability for our current range of supported models, including the ET-X200, ET-X300, ET-S100, and ET-P50. This memorandum outlines the ordering process, expected lead times, and the pricing structure to ensure efficiency and clarity in our operations.

ElectroTech is committed to maintaining a comprehensive inventory of spare parts for our supported models to facilitate quick and reliable service. Our aim is to ensure that parts are readily available for the ET-X200, ET-X300, ET-S100, and ET-P50 models. This availability is in compliance with the Regulation 2025/13 of the Consumer Electronics Parts Management Act, which mandates manufacturers to supply spare parts for a minimum of five years post the discontinuation of a product.

To place an order for spare parts, authorized service centers and partners can access our online Parts Portal, available through the ElectroTech internal network. Orders can also be submitted via email to parts@electrotech.com. For urgent inquiries or assistance, please contact the Parts & Services Department directly at +1-800-555-0199.

Please allow for a standard lead time of 7-10 business days for domestic orders, while international orders may require 14-21 business days, contingent on local customs processes. We have implemented a priority service option that expedites the delivery of critical parts for urgent repairs, reducing lead times by up to 50%. This service is subject to additional fees and availability.

Our pricing structure for spare parts has been revised effective April 1, 2025, under Policy Revision Number SP-2025-04. Spare parts pricing is competitively structured to reflect market trends and is subject to change based on material costs and supply chain fluctuations. These updates will be communicated to all stakeholders on a quarterly basis. For detailed pricing information, authorized personnel can access the updated pricing list on the internal Parts Portal or request it via email at pricing@electrotech.com.

We appreciate your attention to these procedures and look forward to continuing our tradition of service excellence. Please do not hesitate to reach out if you require further clarification or have any questions regarding the spare parts process.
