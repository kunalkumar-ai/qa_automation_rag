To: All ElectroTech Employees  
From: ElectroTech Operations Department  
Date: January 15, 2025  
Subject: Authorised Repair Process for ElectroTech Products

As part of our commitment to providing exceptional customer service and maintaining the high quality of our product offerings, ElectroTech is dedicated to ensuring an efficient and reliable repair process for our esteemed customers. This document outlines the authorised repair process, including booking procedures, expected turnaround times, and courier collection guidelines for our product lines, including the ET-X200, ET-X300, ET-S100, and ET-P50 models.

Customers requiring repairs can initiate the process by contacting our dedicated support team via the online portal or by sending an email to repairs@electrotech.com. It is imperative for customers to provide detailed information, including the product model number, a description of the issue, and proof of purchase, to facilitate a smooth booking process. Our support team will issue a Repair Authorization Number (RAN), which must be included in all subsequent communications and documentation.

For in-warranty repairs, as per policy revision number 2025-01, the turnaround time is five business days from the date the product is received at our service center. This prompt service is a testament to our commitment to customer satisfaction and ensures that our customers can return to enjoying their fully functional products with minimal disruption. Out-of-warranty repairs, on the other hand, have a standard turnaround time of ten business days. Customers will be informed of the estimated cost and must approve the repair charges before any work is commenced.

Our courier collection service is designed to simplify the logistics of sending products to our service centers. Once the RAN is issued, customers can schedule a collection at a convenient time and location. The product must be securely packaged, and the RAN must be prominently displayed on the outside of the package to avoid any delays. ElectroTech has partnered with leading courier companies to ensure safe and efficient transportation of our products, adhering to all relevant safety regulations, including Regulation No. 2025/ET-3.

It is important for all employees to stay informed about these procedures to provide accurate information to our customers. Any updates or changes to the repair process will be communicated through internal memos and updated on our company intranet. For any queries related to the repair process, employees are encouraged to contact the Operations Department at operations@electrotech.com.

By adhering to these guidelines, we aim to preserve the integrity of our products and reinforce the trust our customers place in ElectroTech. Thank you for your continued dedication to excellence.
