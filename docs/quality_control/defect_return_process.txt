To: ElectroTech Employees  
From: Quality Assurance Department  
Date: April 5, 2025  
Subject: Defective Product Return Process and Guidelines

As part of our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction, ElectroTech has established a comprehensive defective product return process. This document outlines the eligibility criteria, the procedure for obtaining a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number, packaging instructions, and refund timelines to ensure a seamless experience for both our customers and our internal teams.

Eligibility for returning a defective product is determined by several factors. Products must be within the one-year warranty period from the date of purchase to qualify for a return. Eligible products include the ET-X200, ET-X300, ET-S100, and ET-P50 models, provided they exhibit manufacturing defects or performance issues not resulting from customer misuse or unauthorized modifications. Customers are required to present a valid proof of purchase, such as a receipt or invoice, for verification purposes.

To initiate the return process, customers must first contact our Customer Support team at returns@electrotech.com to obtain an RMA number. This number is essential for tracking the return and should be clearly marked on the exterior of the return package. The Customer Support team will provide the customer with detailed instructions and support throughout the process, ensuring compliance with the established return policy.

Packaging instructions are critical to prevent further damage during transit. Customers are advised to use the original packaging whenever possible, including all accessories, manuals, and components that accompanied the product. If the original packaging is unavailable, customers should securely package the product in a sturdy box with ample cushioning to protect it during shipment. ElectroTech will not be responsible for damage incurred during return transit due to inadequate packaging.

Upon receipt of the returned product at our facility, the Quality Assurance team will conduct a thorough inspection to confirm the defect and eligibility for a refund. This inspection process typically takes five to seven business days. Following verification, refunds will be processed within 10 to 14 business days. Refunds are issued in the original form of payment unless an alternative arrangement is agreed upon with the customer.

It is crucial that all ElectroTech employees adhere to these procedures to maintain efficiency and uphold our reputation for excellent customer service. For any questions or further clarification regarding the defective product return process, employees are encouraged to contact the Quality Assurance Department at qa@electrotech.com or refer to policy revision number REV-2025-03. Your cooperation and diligence in following these guidelines are greatly appreciated.
