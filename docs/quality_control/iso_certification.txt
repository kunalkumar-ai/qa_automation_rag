Subject: ElectroTech ISO 9001:2015 Certification and Quality Management System

We are pleased to announce that ElectroTech has successfully attained ISO 9001:2015 certification as of February 28, 2025. This certification is a testament to our commitment to quality management and continuous improvement across all our operations. The scope of this certification encompasses the design, manufacturing, and distribution of our electronic products, including but not limited to the ET-X200, ET-X300, and ET-S100 models.

Our ISO 9001:2015 certification ensures that our quality management system (QMS) meets rigorous international standards. The QMS covers all facets of our business processes, with a focus on enhancing customer satisfaction through effective application of the system, including processes for continual improvement and assurance of conformity to customer and applicable statutory and regulatory requirements.

The audit schedule is a critical component of maintaining our certification. Our next surveillance audit is scheduled for September 15, 2025, followed by an annual surveillance audit every year thereafter. These audits will be conducted by our external certification body to ensure ongoing compliance with ISO 9001:2015 standards. Our internal audit team, led by the Quality Assurance Department, will conduct semi-annual audits, with the next internal audit planned for June 2025. These audits will evaluate the effectiveness of our quality management system and identify opportunities for improvement.

The certification is valid for three years, expiring on February 28, 2028, contingent upon passing the annual surveillance audits. ElectroTech is committed to ensuring that our processes and products consistently meet the high-quality standards that our certification demands. This commitment is reflected in our policy revision number 2025-QMS-1, which outlines our updated quality objectives and the roles and responsibilities of staff in maintaining these standards.

We encourage all staff members to familiarize themselves with the updated quality management processes detailed in the policy document available on the company intranet. Your role in upholding these standards is crucial to our success and reputation in the industry.

For any questions or further information regarding our ISO 9001:2015 certification, please contact the Quality Assurance Department at qa@electrotech.com. Thank you for your continued dedication to quality and excellence at ElectroTech.
