Subject: Software Compatibility and Driver Download Guidelines  

As part of our ongoing commitment to ensure seamless functionality and user experience for our customers, we are providing an updated overview of the software compatibility and driver support for our product lines. This document is intended to inform all relevant stakeholders about the current operating system compatibility and the availability of driver downloads for ElectroTech products, including the ET-X200, ET-X300, ET-S100, and ET-P50 models.

As of January 2025, ElectroTech products are fully compatible with the following operating systems: Windows 10 and Windows 11, macOS 13 and later versions, and Ubuntu 22.04. Our software development team has rigorously tested these operating systems to guarantee optimal performance of our products. Customers using any of the specified operating systems can expect reliable and efficient operation without any known critical issues.

For Windows users, it is imperative that their systems are updated with the latest security patches and updates from Microsoft to maintain compatibility with our software. Similarly, macOS users should ensure their systems are running macOS version 13 or newer. Linux users should verify that they are operating on Ubuntu 22.04, as this is the only Linux distribution officially supported at this time.

Driver downloads for all ElectroTech products can be accessed through our official website. We have streamlined the process to facilitate easy access to the necessary drivers, ensuring that customers can quickly and efficiently download and install them. The drivers are available at www.electrotech.com/drivers and are organized by product model for ease of navigation. Our technical support team is available to assist with any issues that may arise during the download or installation process and can be reached at support@electrotech.com.

To further enhance our service, we are committed to updating our compatibility and driver support policies annually. The next scheduled review will occur in January 2026, as part of policy revision number 3.2. During this review process, we will assess the need for additional operating system support and any necessary adjustments to ensure our products remain compatible with future OS releases.

We encourage all employees to familiarize themselves with these guidelines and to assist our customers in understanding the compatibility and driver requirements for their ElectroTech products. For any further inquiries or clarification, please do not hesitate to contact the Software Development Department at software@electrotech.com. We appreciate your cooperation in maintaining the high standards of service that ElectroTech is renowned for.
