In accordance with the international trade regulations and to ensure seamless import and export operations, ElectroTech has established comprehensive protocols to manage the logistics of our products, including the ET-X200, ET-X300, ET-S100, and ET-P50 models. This document serves as a guide for understanding the Harmonized System (HS) codes, country of origin specifications, Economic Operator Registration and Identification (EORI) registration, and Export Control Classification Numbers (ECCNs) for our products.

Firstly, the assignment of HS codes is crucial for determining the tariffs and duties applicable to our products. For instance, the ET-X200 and ET-X300, which are classified under advanced consumer electronics, carry the HS code 8542.39.00, while the ET-S100, a sophisticated home security system, is categorized under HS code 8531.10.00. The portable ET-P50 power unit is assigned the HS code 8507.20.00. These codes are essential for importers and exporters to comply with international trade laws and facilitate smooth customs processing.

The country of origin for all our products is prominently marked and documented as China, where our state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities are located. This information is crucial for customs declarations and to meet the requirements of trade agreements and preferential tariff programs.

ElectroTech is duly registered under the Economic Operator Registration and Identification (EORI) scheme, which is mandatory for companies engaging in import and export activities within the European Union. Our EORI number is GB123456789000 and must be used in all customs documentation for EU transactions. This registration facilitates the identification of our company by customs authorities and is a requisite for ensuring compliance with EU customs regulations.

Additionally, our products are subject to export control classifications to adhere to international security standards. The ET-X200 and ET-X300 models have been classified under ECCN 3A001, indicating they contain controlled technology that requires export licenses for certain countries. Similarly, the ET-S100 is classified under ECCN 5A002 due to its encryption capabilities. The ET-P50, being a commercial power unit, is classified under ECCN EAR99, which generally does not require a license unless exported to embargoed destinations.
