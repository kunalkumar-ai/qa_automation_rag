Subject: ElectroTech Authorized Reseller Brand Guidelines

To all our esteemed authorized resellers,

As a distinguished partner of ElectroTech, it is imperative that you adhere to our brand guidelines to ensure consistency, clarity, and integrity across all communications and marketing efforts. These guidelines have been meticulously crafted to protect the ElectroTech brand and ensure its positive reception in the market.

Firstly, regarding logo usage, the ElectroTech logo is a symbol of quality and innovation, and its correct usage is crucial. Resellers are required to use the logo as provided in the official ElectroTech Brand Kit. The logo must not be altered in any form, including but not limited to color, aspect ratio, or any form of distortion. The standard ElectroTech logo is provided in two formats: ET-LOGO1 (color) and ET-LOGO2 (black and white), which can be requested through marketing@electrotech.com. Ensure that the logo is always surrounded by a minimum clear space equivalent to the height of the 'E' in the logo.

Approved imagery includes product images for the ET-X200, ET-X300, ET-S100, and ET-P50 models. High-resolution images can be accessed through our reseller portal. All images must adhere to the ElectroTech style, characterized by a clean, professional appearance with a white or neutral background. Any requests for custom imagery must be approved by the ElectroTech Marketing Department prior to use.

In terms of co-marketing rules, resellers are encouraged to engage in collaborative marketing activities with ElectroTech, provided all materials are submitted for review at least 15 business days prior to the intended release date. Co-branded materials must prominently feature the ElectroTech logo, adhere to our color palette, and include the statement, "Authorized Reseller of ElectroTech Products," to ensure transparency and trust. Queries and submissions can be directed to co-marketing@electrotech.com.

Resellers must refrain from making prohibited claims. Any statement suggesting modifications to the product or performance enhancements not verified by ElectroTech's official testing is strictly forbidden. Furthermore, claims such as "the best in the world" or "guaranteed 100% flawless" are considered misleading and non-compliant with regulation 2025-ET-RG34. Violations of these terms will result in immediate removal from the authorized reseller program.

These guidelines are part of our ongoing commitment to uphold the highest standards in the electronics industry. The current document supersedes all previous versions and is identified as Policy Revision 2025-01. For further details or clarification, please do not hesitate to contact us at brandguidelines@electrotech.com.

Thank you for your cooperation and dedication to maintaining the esteemed reputation of the ElectroTech brand.

Sincerely,

ElectroTech Brand Management Team
