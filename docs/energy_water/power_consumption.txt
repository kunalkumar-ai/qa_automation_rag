ElectroTech is committed to manufacturing high-quality electronic products with a focus on energy efficiency and compliance with global environmental standards. As part of this commitment, we continuously evaluate and update the power consumption specifications for our product lines, including the ET-X200, ET-X300, ET-S100, and ET-P50 models. This document outlines the power consumption details and the ErP compliance status for these models, effective as of January 2025.

The ET-X200 model has been designed with energy efficiency in mind, featuring a standby power consumption of 0.5 watts and an active consumption of 20 watts. Annually, this model is estimated to consume approximately 175 kWh based on average user operation patterns. Similarly, the ET-X300 model, which offers enhanced features, maintains a standby consumption of 0.6 watts and an active consumption of 25 watts, resulting in an estimated annual consumption of 210 kWh.

The ET-S100, our streamlined option for basic electronic needs, demonstrates our commitment to sustainability with a standby consumption of a mere 0.4 watts and an active consumption of 15 watts. This results in an annual consumption estimate of 130 kWh. Finally, our compact ET-P50 model, designed for portable use, operates with a standby consumption of 0.3 watts and an active consumption of 10 watts, equating to an annual usage of approximately 95 kWh.

In compliance with the Energy-related Products Directive (ErP), all our models meet the stringent requirements set forth in Regulation (EU) 2019/1782. This regulation, which governs the eco-design of electronic products, ensures that our products not only meet but exceed minimum energy efficiency thresholds. Each model is tested rigorously to conform to these standards, ensuring that they contribute to reduced energy consumption and environmental impact.

ElectroTech regularly reviews and updates its product specifications to align with advancements in technology and regulatory changes. Our current policy revision, identified as version 3.2.1, was implemented to ensure full compliance with the latest ErP guidelines and to optimize the energy consumption of our products further.
