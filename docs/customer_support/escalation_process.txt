ElectroTech is committed to providing outstanding customer support to ensure the satisfaction and loyalty of our clients. To achieve this, we have established a structured customer support escalation process, designed to efficiently address and resolve any issues that may arise with our products, such as the ET-X200, ET-X300, ET-S100, and ET-P50 models. This document outlines the escalation tiers and the corresponding service level agreements (SLAs) associated with each tier.

Tier 1 Support serves as the initial point of contact for customer inquiries and issues. Customers can reach our Tier 1 Support team via email at support@electrotech.com. Our team is dedicated to promptly addressing customer concerns and providing solutions within a 48-hour timeframe, in accordance with our SLA policy. This tier is primarily responsible for handling general product inquiries, basic troubleshooting, and providing information on product features and specifications. Should a customer's issue require more specialized attention or fail to be resolved within the provided timeframe, it will be escalated to Tier 2 Support.

Tier 2 Support offers a more personalized and immediate response, as customers engage with our knowledgeable support staff via phone. This tier is equipped to handle more complex technical issues that require a deeper understanding of our products, including the ET-X300 and ET-S100. The phone support team is committed to resolving issues within a 24-hour SLA. Customers can contact Tier 2 Support by calling our dedicated helpline at 1-800-555-ETEC. If an issue remains unresolved or requires further escalation, it will be directed to Tier 3 Support.

Tier 3 Support involves our management team, providing the highest level of customer service for critical issues that demand urgent attention. This tier is designated for exceptional cases, such as unresolved technical problems with the ET-P50, or situations involving compliance with industry regulations, such as Regulation No. 2025/ET-REG. Our management team is dedicated to delivering swift resolutions within a 4-hour SLA. Customers who require Tier 3 Support will be contacted directly by a member of our management team to ensure that their concerns are addressed promptly and effectively.

Our escalation process is integral to maintaining the high standards of customer service that ElectroTech is known for. We continually review and refine our support policies, with the next scheduled revision (Policy Revision No. CS-2025-01) set for January 15, 2025. For further details on our customer support escalation process or to provide feedback, please email us at feedback@electrotech.com. ElectroTech remains steadfast in its commitment to excellence in customer service and satisfaction.
